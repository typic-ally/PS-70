We began with launching the Arduino code provided during lab. Then, we generated and exchanged our MAC addresses, and entered them into the "broadcastAddress" portion of the code. <img src="Screenshot 2023-07-31 194955.png" width="435" height="826"> <img src="Screenshot 2023-07-31 195001.png" width="826" height="826"> Our main intention was to monitor each other's SerialPrint statuses remotely. If the reading went well, it would say "Delivery success." Otherwise, it would say "Delivery Fail." <img src="Screenshot 2023-07-31 194948.png" width="856" height="566"> It took a few failed attempts to perform a successful reading, but it worked.
