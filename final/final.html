<br> #### Final Project By: Allison Fairman For my Final Project, I created a machine that can determine the moisture level of a plant's soil and distribute the necessary amount of water. To build this project, I used: - A Waterpump - Capacitive soil sensor - Empty spray-bottle container - Extra tubes - Plywood (5 lasercut pieces) - Socket-Socket wires (Double socket) - Double-sided wires - Socket-wire (One socket) - 3 mini breadboards - Arduino board & cord - Programmable push-button - 2 Oled display screens (SSD1306 I2C Oled display) - LED strip I scaled measured it using the UCP software's top and left measurement feature. It's better to scale it in the Rhino software, while also making sure the width and height are at least 24cm or more.
