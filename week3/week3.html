<br> #### Week 3: Electronics and Tools I created a simple, rotating sunflower for my kinetic sculpture project using wood and a little cardboard. I built two distinct flower shapes in Fusion 360 and glued them on top of them other to give it a more floral look. Here's a tutorial video on how I made the shape: <video width="720" height="520" controls> <source src="Autodesk Fusion 360.mp4" type="video/mp4"> </video> <img src="sunflower lasercut design.png" width="620" height="310"> I exported this sketch as a DXF, measured the wood at 3.08mm, and lasercut it. Then, I sketched a rectangle and a square for the base that holds the sunflower together. Finally, I duplicated one of the squares and sized it down to a smaller one. <img src="sunflower base design.png" width="640" height="310"> After lasercutting, this is how my project turned out: <img src="kinetic sculpture 1.png" width="621" height="623"> <img src="kinetic sculpture 2.png" width="629" height="636"> <img src="kinetic sculpture 3.png" width="554" height="559"> Here is the video of the kinetic sculpture turning: <video width="720" height="520" controls> <source src="IMG_0450.mov" type="video/mp4"> </video>
